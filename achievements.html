Our ACHIVEMENTS

At Boberick the Ilama Institute, we are dedicated to preparing our students for successful careers in the fast-paced and ever-changing field of technology. We are proud to recognize the following achievements of our students and faculty:

  1. Our cybersecurity program was recently ranked in the top 10% of cybersecurity programs nationwide by Cyber Degrees EDU, recognizing the high quality of our curriculum, faculty, and student outcomes.
  2. A team of our computer science students recently won first place in a regional hackathon, where they developed an innovative app that simplifies the process of scheduling appointments.
  3. One of our faculty members was recently awarded a research grant from the National Science Foundation to study artificial intelligence and machine learning, contributing to the cutting-edge research being conducted at ABC Technology Institute.
  4. A group of our engineering students designed and built a solar-powered vehicle that competed in a national competition, placing in the top 5 in their category and demonstrating their ingenuity and technical skills.
  5. One of our alumni was recently hired as the Chief Technology Officer for a major tech startup, reflecting the caliber of our graduates and the strong connections we maintain with industry leaders.

These achievements demonstrate the strength and excellence of our programs and the dedication of our faculty and students to advancing the field of technology. We are committed to providing our students with the skills, knowledge, and experience they need to succeed in this exciting and rapidly evolving industry.
